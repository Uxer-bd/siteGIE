Formation en Initiative Personnelle (PI Training)

Le Personal Initiative (PI) Training est un programme de formation orienté vers l'action et basé sur des preuves, qui se concentre sur la création d'un état d'esprit entrepreneurial et a prouvé son efficacité pour augmenter le succès entrepreneurial.

Doorways est un fournisseur certifié de la formation PI.

Detail Pi Impact

The impact of Personal Initiative (PI) Training is manifold. First, it changes the mindset of entrepreneurs, enabling them to identify new opportunities, act quickly, develop better ideas on how to influence their environment, get more feedback, and persist in the pursuit of their new ideas. Second, by providing effective entrepreneurship training in low- and middle-income economies, we support the growth of local businesses and contribute to poverty reduction. Third, we help policymakers and practitioners develop better educational programs for entrepreneurs. The PI Training is based on scientific evidence and meets the highest quality standards by utilizing randomized controlled trials (RCTs). RCTs allow us to draw causal conclusions and better understand the conditions and mechanisms by which entrepreneurship training affects business success.

PI Training has been implemented in various internationally funded research projects in Africa, Asia, Latin America, and the Caribbean. Its impact is evaluated using multiple measurement waves. In Togo, for example, its impact was assessed over more than two years. Below, you will find some facts and figures from Leuphana University's research in Uganda and Togo (see Glaub et al., 2014; Campos et al., 2017).
